The Argentinian also have the most individual awards in history.He have won the Balon do'r 8 times,the golden boot 6 times,he was named the worlds best player for a record 8 times by FIFA.Messi started his senior career in Barcelona where he created most of his Legacy and the moved to Paris in 2021 and now he is curently in the MLS to seal his beautiful journey in football.

Messi has a total of 826 career goals which is few behind his long term rival Cristiano Ronaldo.A prolific goalscorer and a creative playmaker, Messi holds the record of the most goals for a single club(672),he has the most goals in La Liga(474),the most hattricks in La Liga(36).
